The Crew Perfume

This work embodies the essence of the brand—sophistication, luxury and exclusivity. The logo features a sleek, modern typography that exudes a sense of refined elegance. The color palette of deep navy blue combined with accents of gold reflects the brand's premium and luxurious appeal.

Objective:

Our objective was to create a distinctive brand identity for The Crew Perfume that reflects the allure, sophistication, and travel-inspired spirit of the airplane crew.

We believe that fragrance is not merely a scent; it is an invitation to embark on a sensory voyage, an olfactory adventure.
