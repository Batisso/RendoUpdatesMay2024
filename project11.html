Zadulis

Zadulis, established in 2024, is a Platform to discover unique African creative goods and products! It is dedicated to showcasing and promoting the rich heritage, artistry, and craftsmanship of Africa. The platform serves as a vibrant hub where artisans, designers, and creators from all corners of Africa gather to share their unique creations with the world.


Date

2024

Role

Branding

Scroll to Explore

Objective:

Our objective was to create a brand identity for Zadulis that captures the essence of African creativity while resonating with a global audience. We aimed to design a brand that evokes a sense of discovery, authenticity, and appreciation for the rich cultural heritage of Africa. Our goal was to establish Zadulis as the go-to platform for discovering and acquiring one-of-a-kind African creative goods.

Brand Approach:

To develop the brand identity for Zadulis, we embarked on a journey of exploration, cultural immersion, and creative collaboration. Our team drew inspiration from the vibrant colors, patterns, and symbols found in African art and textiles.
We combined these elements with modern design aesthetics to create a brand that is both contemporary and deeply rooted in African heritage.
