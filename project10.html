Hill Bottom Billboards

Hill Bottom Real Estate is a prime address for residents in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia looking for spacious apartments, exclusive amenities and a community lifestyle in a picturesque village setting. A luxury apartment and exceptional amenities that create an indulgent residential experience in a charming neighborhood environment.


Date

2024

Role

BillBoards Graphic Design, Color Pallete, & Typography

Scroll to Explore

Objective:

Our objective was to design a billboard that would grab attention, leave a lasting impression, and convey the unique value proposition of Hill Bottom Real Estate. We aimed to create a visually striking design that would make a statement, evoke emotions, and inspire individuals to trust Hill Bottom as their go-to real estate partner.

Design Approach:

To create an impactful billboard design, we adopted a combination of creativity, strategic messaging, and captivating visuals. Our design team meticulously crafted a composition that would immediately catch the eye, while effectively communicating Hill Bottom Real Estate's core values, exceptional service, and extensive property portfolio.

Results and Impact:

The captivating billboard design has significantly increased Hill Bottom Real Estate's brand visibility and generated a surge in inquiries and leads. The eye-catching design has successfully captured the attention of passersby, creating instant brand recall and establishing Hill Bottom Real Estate as a reliable and trustworthy real estate partner.
